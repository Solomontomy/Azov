Our End-to-End Process

We manage the entire lifecycle of your shade structure, from design to durable installation.

01

Steel Fabrication

In-house welding and finishing of structural steel frames, treated with anti-corrosion coatings for long-term durability in the UAE climate.

02

Fabric Membrane Production

Precision cutting and stitching of architectural fabrics (HDPE, PVC, PTFE) to ensure perfect fit, UV resistance, and maximum shade coverage.

03

Erection & Safety

Installation by certified teams following strict safety protocols, ensuring all foundation bolts and fasteners are secure and protected against corrosion.
